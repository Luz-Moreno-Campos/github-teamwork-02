Building fot the web involves more than writing codes, it's about creating experiences that are functional, accessible, and maintainable.

Web Accessiblity

The web is designed to work for all people, regardless of their hardware software, language, location, or ability. Semantic HTML is the first step toward accessiblity.

"The power of the Web is in its universality. Access by everyone regardless of disability is an essential aspect." — Tim Berners-Lee
